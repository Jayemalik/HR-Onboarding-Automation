Contoso offers a wide-ranging benefits package for employees, with some variation depending on your specific Office Location.

For the Contoso-Guangzhou office, the benefits include:

  • Tier 1 Health Insurance Coverage
  • Contributions to your Retirement Plan of 2% of your salary
  • A FitnessPerks benefit of up to $600 per year
  • Access to the Contoso-Guangzhou gymnasium complex
  • Access to the Contoso-Guangzhou legal advisory services for a variety of issue types
