Contoso offers a wide-ranging benefits package for employees, with some variation depending on your specific Office Location.

For the Contoso-NewYork office, the benefits include:

  • Tier 1 Health Insurance Coverage
  • Contributions to your Retirement Plan of 8% of your salary
  • A FitnessPerks benefit of up to $1000 per year
  • A discount of 50% on any merchandise from the Contoso company store
  • Access to the Contoso legal advisory services for a variety of issue types
